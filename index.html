For Participants

Registration
Composers who are willing to participate in the competition must submit the application form By June 5th 23:59 (EET), 2020.
Composers who register before April 5th 23:59 (EET), 2020 have to pay an entry fee of 40 Eur.
Those who apply to the competition after this deadline must pay a fee of 70 Eur.
The registration fee is returned to the composer only in the case of competition being cancelled.
By sending the application you agree with the Regulations of the competition. You will find the application form by clicking here.

Score deadline
Every registered participant will be contacted and asked to submit by July 5th 23:59 (EET), 2020:
  • a composition score in PDF format (requirements of the score can be found here)
  • a MIDI or an MP3 audio file (created with a music notation software).
For more information please see the regulations of the competition.

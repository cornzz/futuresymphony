October 8th, 2018 - 08:00 pm
Results of the 1st competition final round
We are excited to announce the winners of the 1st international music composition competition "Future Symphony"!
1st place: 🇨🇳️ Xiaoran Jiang ("Befall")
2nd place: 🇰🇷️ SiHyun Uhm ("Apex of Longing"), 🇺🇸️ Robert Ge ("Night Run")
3rd place: 🇨🇳️ Pengyi Li ("Polymorphism")
Audience Sympathy prize: 🇱🇹️ Matas Drukteinis ("Late Night at the City")
Biggest congratulations to the composers!

Compositions were evaluated by the jury:
Marius Baranauskas (chairman)
Gediminas Gelgotas
Ayal Adler
Rasa Murauskaitė
Modestas Barkauskas.
The competition featured composers from Lithuania, Estonia, Georgia, France, USA, Canada, China, Taiwan, Australia and South Korea.

For Participants

Registration
Composers who are willing to participate in the competition must submit:
By June 5th 23:59 (EET), 2020:
  • the application form
  • a copy of passport / ID card
  • a good quality (at least the size of 1 MB) photo of the participant
  • a copy of payment confirmation.
Composers who register before April 5th 23:59 (EET), 2020 have to pay an entry fee of 40 Eur.
Those who apply to the competition after this deadline must pay a fee of 70 Eur.
The registration fee is returned to the composer only in the case of competition being cancelled.
You will find the application form by clicking here. By sending the application you agree with the Regulations of the competition.

Score deadline
By July 5th 23:59 (EET), 2020 participants must submit:
  • a composition score in PDF format (requirements of the score can be found here)
  • a MIDI or an MP3 audio file (created with a music notation software).

Results of the final round

September 18th, 2020 - 07:00 pm

We are excited to announce the winners of the 2nd international music composition competition "Future Symphony"!

1st place:
🇲🇽 Charles Philip Daniels Torres ("The Myth of Déremot-Bélel")

2nd place:
🇱🇹 Jievaras Jasinskis ("Change")

3rd place:
🇩🇪🇵🇱 Roman Czura ("Rythms Drowned in Dreams")
🇱🇹 Andrius Maslekovas ("Waters-Reflections-Haiku")

Saulius Karosas special prize for the best composition of the Lithuanian author:
🇱🇹 Jievaras Jasinskis ("Change")

Audience Sympathy prize:
🇭🇺 Apor Szüts ("Rondo una quasi toccata")

Orchestra Sympathy prize:
🇭🇺 Apor Szüts ("Rondo una quasi toccata")


Also, the following pieces were performed:

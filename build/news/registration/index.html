Registration to the "Future Symphony" competition starts in February

January 18th, 2020 - 3:00 pm

We are delighted to announce the registration to the 2nd international music composition competition for symphony orchestra "Future Symphony". Applications are welcome from February 5th until June 5th 2020. Composers from all around the world at the age of 18-35 can apply to the competition.

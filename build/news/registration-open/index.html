Registration starts now, final instrumentation is announced!

February 5th, 2020 - 5:00 pm

We are excited to announce that the registration to the „Future Symphony” competition is now open! The applications from all over the world are welcome till the 5th of June 2020. Register here now! We would like to remind you that application fee until the 5th of April 2020 is 50 eur, from April 6th 2020 onwards is 80 EUR.

Also we inform you about the slight changes of the orchestration for the application score. You can find the final instrumentation limits for this years competition here.

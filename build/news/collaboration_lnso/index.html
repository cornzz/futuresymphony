Collaboration with LNSO

April 11th, 2022 - 8:00 pm
This year the Grand Prix winning composition will be performed by the Lithuanian National Symphony Orchestra (LNSO) in the season 2022–2023.

This exclusive opportunity is made possible by Mr. Modestas Pitrenas, Artistic Director and Principal Conductor of the LNSO, and Mrs. Rūta Prusevičienė, General Director of the Lithuanian National Philharmonic Society (Filharmonija).
